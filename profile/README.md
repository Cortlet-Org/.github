## What We Build
We build developer tools, open-source/closed-source tools, etc
These are made with the Cortlet Philosiphy:
- Lightweight
- Secure-first
- No-bloat
- Problem-solving.
Also known as the LSNP philosiphy, Cortlet delivers products designed for developers, and normal users.
